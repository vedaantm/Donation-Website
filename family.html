Tour details

Day 1: New York

  • Arrive at New York. Check-In in the Roosevelt Hotel.
  • Go for an evening stroll to Central Park
  • Go back to Hotel for dinner.

Day 2: New York

  • After early morning after breakfast go for sightseeing of New York city .
  • 9/11 memorial,Empire state Building & ferry ride to Statue of Liberty in Liberty Island .
  • Lunch Break
  • In the evening,drive to the One World Trade Centre. After witnessing the city from the top, proceed forward for a heart-filling shopping session.
  • Later, drive across the Times Square, and the Manhattan city before returning back to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Day 3:New York to WashingtonDC

  • After Breakfast at hotel, depart to airport to WashingtonDC.
  • Lunch Break
  • Check-In in the Hotel Hay-Adams.
  • In the evening,head out for sight-seeing begin with a visit to the Washington Monument followed by Lincoln Memorial, White House, and Smithsonian Air and Space Museum.
  • Back to Hotel for sightseeing.

Day 4: WashingtonDC to Niagra

  • After breakfast, check-out from hotel.
  • Drive to Hershey's Chocolate World to enjoy a chocolate adventure
  • Lunch Break
  • Later, drive across the border to reach Niagara. On arrival, check-in at the Check -In the hotel Marriot on the Falls.

Day 5: Niagra

  • After breakfast,gear up for sightseeing tour of Niagara Falls.
  • After that explore other attractions such as Goat Island, Cave of the Winds, along with a 'Maid of the Mist' cruise tour which provides close encounters with the falls.
  • Lunch Break
  • Later, proceed to visit the Skylon Tower. Also, witness the Niagara falls illuminations in the evening.
  • Back to Hotel for dinner

Day 6:Niagra to Chicago

  • Early morning after breakfast,check-out from hotel & fly to Chicago
  • Arrive in Chicago, Check-In the Hotel 166 .
  • Lunch Break
  • Later, embark on a Chicago Architecture boat tour followed by city orientation tour which includes visits to places like Swami Vivekananda way, Buckingham fountain, Claude Gate, and Michigan Avenue.
  • Later visit Lake Michigan waterfront, and the University of Chicago before returning to the hotel..

Day 7:Chicago to Orlando

  • After early breakfast, fly to Orlando.
  • Arrive in Orlando, Check -In at Hotel Hilto Orlando.
  • The remaining part of the day is free for you to shop at the International Premium Outlets.
  • Get back to hotel for dinner.

Day 7:Orlando

  • After early breakfast go for guided tour to KSC & Magic Kindom.
  • After full day activity go back to hotel for dinner

Day 8:Orlando to Home

  • After early breakfastdrive to airport for flying to home. Tour Conclude!
